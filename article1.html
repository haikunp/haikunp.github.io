Understanding Blockchain Technology

Blockchain technology is the backbone of cryptocurrencies like Bitcoin and Ethereum. It is a decentralized ledger that records all transactions across a network of computers. This ensures transparency and security, as each transaction is verified by multiple nodes in the network.

How Does Blockchain Work?

When a transaction is made, it is grouped with other transactions into a block. This block is then added to the chain of previous blocks, hence the name "blockchain." Each block contains a unique code called a hash, which ensures the integrity of the data.

Benefits of Blockchain

  • Decentralization: No single entity controls the blockchain, making it resistant to censorship and fraud.
  • Transparency: All transactions are publicly recorded, ensuring accountability.
  • Security: The use of cryptographic hashes makes it nearly impossible to alter past transactions.

Blockchain technology has the potential to revolutionize various industries, from finance to supply chain management. By understanding how it works, you can better appreciate the innovation behind cryptocurrencies.
